Research Questions & Findings

a) Does the price vary depending on the airline?

Yes, flight prices vary significantly depending on the airline. Some airlines consistently offer higher average prices than others.

b) How does the price change when tickets are booked 1 or 2 days before departure?

Prices tend to be higher when tickets are booked close to the departure date. Early booking is generally cheaper.

c) Does the price vary based on departure and arrival times?

Yes, departure and arrival times have a moderate impact. Late-night and early-morning flights are usually cheaper.

d) How does the price change according to source and destination cities?

Source and destination cities have a limited impact on price variations compared to other features.

e) How does the ticket price differ between Economy and Business class?

Business class tickets are significantly more expensive than Economy class tickets, making class one of the strongest price factors.

Quick Conclusion

This project demonstrates that flight ticket prices are mainly influenced by travel class, airline, flight duration, number of stops, and booking time. Business class and longer flights significantly increase prices, while early booking and direct flights tend to reduce costs.

The analysis and machine learning models confirm strong predictive patterns in the dataset, making it suitable for accurate price prediction and classification tasks.
